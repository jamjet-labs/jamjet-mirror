JamJet

The safety layer that sits under your AI agents.

Keep LangGraph, CrewAI, Spring AI, or Claude Code. Add JamJet for policy, approval, audit, durability, and memory. Agent failures are infrastructure failures, not model failures, and that is the layer JamJet owns.

Three pillars

OSS Runtime

Open-source engine in Rust, Python, and Java. Block unsafe tool calls, require approval, enforce budgets, audit, and replay.

JamJet Cloud

Hosted control plane: observe agent runs, enforce governance, and prove what happened with a durable audit trail.

Engram

Agent memory. A temporal knowledge graph that gives agents durable, queryable long-term context.
